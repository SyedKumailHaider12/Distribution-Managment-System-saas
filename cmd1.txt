PART 4 — PRINT TRIGGER INTEGRATION
================================================================================

File: src/app/sales/SalesClient.tsx

After successful createSalesInvoice():
  - Retail:
      1. Fetch full invoice data (with org info) using getSalesInvoiceById().

  - Distribution:
      1. Fetch full invoice data.
      2. Use @react-pdf/renderer's pdf() to generate blob.
      3. Open in new tab via URL.createObjectURL() for print/download.
